[Hook 1]
Naina de jaadu naal kardi tu mind blow
Tera nakhra wakhra, jag challe slow
Sohne mukhre de utte tere penda moonlight
Dil karda ae mera tainu vekha all night

[Hook 2]
Main aashiq tera haan tu meri heartthrob
Tainu takkda haan karda na koyi hor job
Main karda haan pyar tainu without a doubt
Ni tu jadu jiha pawe jadon aavein tu out

[Verse 1]
Kaali raat vich chamke tera mukhra bright
Tere piche main ghumda haan chasing the light
Kade aavein kade jaavein tu kheddi game
Mere naina vich disda ae only your name
Main sehanda haan nakhre tere all the way
Tutda ae dil jadon kardi na stay
Main uchi udaari maran sky is the limit
Tainu apna banavanga in just a minute

[Pre-Chorus]
Tere sahaan di khushboo ne kitta behaal
Meri dhadkan te challe tera hi taal
Ik waari mainu dede tu little sign
Main aashiq haan tera, please be mine

[Chorus]
Ni Havana di raatan ch disda tera noor
Tainu labhda main firaan, baby don't go door
Mera dil bekabu hunda vekh tera glow
Tere piche main bhajjda, I can never let go
Ni Havana di raatan ch disda tera noor
Tere haseyaan ne kitta mainu bada majboor
Sari sari raat chalda ae passion deep
Tainu bhul nahio sakda even in sleep

[Verse 2]
Tez hawawan ch udde tera hair
Nakhre vikhaundi ae tu like you don't care
Tere piche chadheya main poora sansaar
Main karda haan tenu true wala pyar
Kade zahar wargi tu toxic romance
Par labhda haan tera ik loving glance
Paiye ghumde ne raahan te leaving a mark
Tere bina rovan kalla in the dark
